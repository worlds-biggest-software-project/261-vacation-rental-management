## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features

- Channel management (Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com, etc.)
- Dynamic pricing and rate management
- Guest communication and messaging
- Cleaning and maintenance scheduling
- Booking calendar and availability management
- Financial reporting and invoicing
- Guest reviews and reputation management
- Property listing management

### Differentiating Features

- AI-powered autonomous pricing (Beyond, PriceLabs)
- Digital guest guidebooks (Hostfully)
- Predictive cleaning and maintenance scheduling
- Hyper-personalised guest messaging (LLM-based)
- Fraud and chargeback prevention
- Regulatory compliance automation (tax, permits)
- AI-first guest communication
- Enterprise integrations and APIs

### Underserved Areas

- End-to-end regulatory compliance (tax, permits, licenses)
- Predictive maintenance and damage prevention
- Fraud and chargeback prevention at scale
- Real-time operational anomaly detection
- Competitor pricing intelligence and adaptation
- Automated guest experience optimisation

### AI-Augmentation Candidates

- Autonomous revenue management (pricing, competitor monitoring)
- Predictive cleaning and maintenance scheduling
- Hyper-personalised guest communication
- Regulatory compliance automation
- Fraud prevention and guest screening
- Dynamic pricing with constraint optimization
